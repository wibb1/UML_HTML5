Our mission statement

We here at Susie's Signs are devoted to making signs that stand out, stand up, and stand the test of time. If you need a sign for interior or exterior use, just let us know and we will work with you to design and make the sign that fits your vision.

The signs that are shown as examles are just some of the ideas that we have made happen for our many clients. Bring your ideas to light is our business, if you don't see something that is appeals to you, feel free to use our design tool here. Design Tool or email us a design of your own. Email us

Equipment Capabilities

Traditional WoodWorking

We are capable of many things, traditional woodworking techniques from hand tooling to power equipment are definitely in our line of duty. If you would like a truely hand crafted masterpiece we can do that. There are many techniques that cannot be reproduced by the available shop equipment, even in a well equiped shop like ours. We will work with you to get the most cost effective method without breaking your budget.


Laser Engraving

The technique of laser engraving brings many new abilites to a sign shop. The ability to engrave a photograph, like in the video below, is one of the more impressive.

CNC Routing

CNC routing has been around for a long time, but the recent movement in the industry toward smaller equipment has brouhgt big changes to Susie's Signs. The repeatability and ability to make each side identicle appeals to people who would like to have many of the same signs made or would like to have carving that the cost of a handmade sign becomes exorbitant.

Recent Projects

Susie's Signs is committed to making your project come out just the way you want it. We make sure you are involved the entire way and all of your ideas are always taken into account. I hope that you can see that what we build and the way that we build it are reflected in the final product.

The Johnsons

Working with Sarah Johnson, we designed this sign for her cabin in Vermont. She wanted a sign that guests would notice when coming down the road to her secluded gravel driveway but not spoil the drive for others. She loved her sign and we loved working with her.

The Huntleys

Longtime friends of ours, the Huntleys were one of our first customers. They came back to us in 2019 when they wanted a sign made from some of the wood harvested from their property in New Hampshire. We were happy to help! The wood was rough sawn when it came in but we were able to produce a great piece of art for them to hang at their house in Massachusetts.
